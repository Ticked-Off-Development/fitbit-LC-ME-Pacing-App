# Fitbit App Gallery Description

Use this description when submitting to the Fitbit App Gallery (max 1000 characters).

---

A pacing assistant for people with Long Covid or ME (Myalgic Encephalomyelitis). LC ME Pacing monitors your heart rate in real-time and alerts you with haptic vibrations when you exceed your Anaerobic Threshold (AT) — helping you Stop, Rest, & Pace.

Features:
- Multiple AT formulas: WorkWell (RHR+15), 50/55/60% of Max HR, or custom
- Configurable haptic alerts with adjustable interval and type
- Mute/snooze button to temporarily silence alerts
- HR trend indicator showing rising, falling, or stable direction
- AT Stats page: time above AT, recovery timer, and daily exertion budget
- Daily exertion budget with color-coded progress bar and 80% warning
- Color-coded HR zones (blue, green, yellow, orange, red)
- Battery and time display

All data is processed locally on your device. No data is collected or transmitted. See our privacy policy for details.

Inspired by the HRM4Pacing community.
